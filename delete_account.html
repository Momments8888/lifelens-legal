Delete Your LifeLens AI Account & Data

For the LifeLens AI app, published by LifeLens AI LLC · Last updated: July 17, 2026

This page explains how to delete your LifeLens AI account and the data associated with it, what gets deleted, and what (if anything) is retained.

Option 1 — Delete your account inside the app (fastest)

  1. Open the LifeLens AI app and sign in.
  2. Go to Profile (bottom-right tab).
  3. Tap Privacy.
  4. Tap "Delete my account & wipe data".
  5. Confirm the deletion. Your account and all associated data are scheduled for permanent removal immediately.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can no longer access the app, email privacy@lifelens.ai from the email address associated with your LifeLens account with the subject line "Delete my account". We will verify ownership and complete the deletion within 30 days, and confirm by email when it is done.

What is deleted

When your account is deleted, all of the following data is permanently and irreversibly deleted from our servers:

  • Your account (email address, name, login credentials)
  • All memories: text notes, voice-note transcriptions, photos, and imported email/text/PDF content
  • All AI questions and answers (Ask AI history)
  • Location data: memory location tags, saved parking spots, and location reminders
  • Health data: imported step counts, distance, calories, and heart-rate summaries
  • Family sharing memberships and shared timeline entries you created
  • Subscription status records (payment card details are held by Stripe, never by us)

Retention periods

DataRetention after deletion request
Account & all data listed abovePermanently deleted within 30 days
Encrypted server backupsPurged automatically within an additional 30 days (max 60 days total)
Billing records required by tax/accounting lawRetained by Stripe per legal requirements; contains no memory content

Deleting some data without deleting your account

You do not have to delete your whole account to remove data:

  • Individual memories: open any memory → tap Delete. Deleted memories move to Trash for 30 days (so you can restore them), then are permanently erased — or empty the Trash to erase them immediately.
  • Location data: disable Smart Recall in Profile at any time; delete individual location-tagged memories as above.
  • Health data: disconnect Health Connect / Apple Health in the app, and email us to wipe previously imported health summaries.
  • Or email privacy@lifelens.ai describing the specific data you want removed.
Questions? Contact privacy@lifelens.ai. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.
